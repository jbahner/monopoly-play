Rules of Monopoly

General Procedure

Monopoly is played with 2 to 6 players, each starting with 1500€ budget. The first action of a turn is to move the token forward on the files by the number rolled on the dice. When landing on an unowned property, the player can decide to buy it for the given price. If the property is already bought by another player, he has to pay rent as listed on the property. If the property is already owned by the player stepping on it, nothing happens.

Fields and Properties

Go!

As you could imagine, this is the start field for every player. When passing this field, the player is given 200€. Other than that, nothing happens if you land on the fiel itself.

Taxes

There are 2 tax fiels on the board. When landing on a tax field, the player has to pay the amount to the Bank. The income tax at the beginning of the board is 100€, the luxury tax at the end is 200€.

Jail

If you simply step on the Jail, nothing happens, since you are only visiting. If you somehow must go to Jail, you will have to roll a double to get out again. On each turn, you have 3 rolls to receive the double. Alternatively, you can pay 500€ to the Bank instead of rolling. When in Jail, you will still receive rent and you are allowed to buy houses.

Free Parking

On Free Parking, nothing happens. It is just a field without any effects.

Go To Jail

This field already states its function alredy in its name. If you land on the "Go To Jail" field, you must immediately go to jail.

Chance and Community Chest

When landing on a Chance or Community Chest field, the player draws a card from the corresponding card stack. He then has to follow the instructions written on it.

Properties

Streets

Streets are the most fields on the board. They are in coloured groups of 2 or 3. If a player has all the streets of a group, he has several advantages: The base rent of each street is doubled and he is allowed to build houses. The rent of a street is determined by the amount of houses built on it.

Stations

There are 4 stations on the board. The rent when landing on one of them is determined by the amount of stations bought by the owner.

Works

There is the electricity and the water works. When landing on those fields, the rent is 4 times the rolled dice if the player owns one of them and 10 times the rolled dice if the player owns both.
